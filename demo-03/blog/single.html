Our Super-Fast Drying Shorts

July 21th, 2017

Our unique matching father and son swim shorts come in eight truly tropical designs. These playful prints don’t just look cool, they’re made from fast drying high-performance polyester fabric and they are super comfy. What exactly makes our shorts dry so fast? Read on to find out.

Super Synthetics

Clothes designed for sporting or leisure activities should breathe well, hold moisture away from the skin and dry quickly. Nylon and polyester are two synthetic materials that are perfect for this task which is why they are usually the base for most performance fabrics. Polyester was developed and subsequently introduced to the public between the 1930s and 1950s. Hailed at the time as a ‘miracle fabric’ for its crinkle-free and durable properties, polyester has recently been enjoying a comeback.

Spin an Old Yarn

Synthetic materials have a surprisingly long history. As far back as the early 1800s, inventors have been experimenting with modifying materials. ‘Artificial silk’, for example, was created by dissolving the inner bark of a mulberry tree to create a cellulose solution. Other inventors made machines that imitated silkworm production, forcing liquid tree-bark through the holes to create threads of material similar to rayon.

Our Brilliant Beachwear

Splashing about in a paddling pool or basking at the beach, you want beachwear that is comfy, in and out of the water. At Robert and Son, we believe in quality and comfort. All of our swim shorts are manufactured from 100% polyester fast drying fabric so you can look cool and keep cool.

Fabric Features

  • Polyester fabrics are strong and durable
  • Polyester is “hydrophobic” making it quick drying and mildew resistant
  • Polyester is crease-proof, easy to wash and dry
