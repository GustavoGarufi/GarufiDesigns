Free Delivery

Every order that is shipped to the UK is elegible for free first class shipping by following the steps below.

1

Step One

Start building you package, you can do this by going into our product page and filling up your cart with all the goodies you will need.

2

Step Two

After you've gotten your selection of swim shorts, you can now take your cart and order your package.

3

Step Three

When asked the shipping address for your order, simply select in the country section "UK". The system will automatically give free shipping!
