Our Story

Sail away on adventures and make memories that will last a lifetime with our colourful range of father and son swim shorts in bold, playful prints.

History

Robert and Son is a family run business inspired by the magic of holidays spent sailing, surfing and hanging out at the beach. Father of two sons, Marc created the business after trying – and failing – to find matching father and son shorts at a reasonable price.

With his background in global commodities, Marc knew what it takes to develop a product like this, so he decided to do it himself. Now, Robert and Son is a rising beachwear brand – although Marc’s sons are still his biggest fans.
