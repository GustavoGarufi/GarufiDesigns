About Us

Freshly prepared focaccia’s made the old-school italian way, using only real ingredients. And when they’re served in a laid- back space designed for relaxation, there’s no mistaking our focacceria for anything short of real authentic.
At Made in Italy, we serve the secrets of Manuelina’s focaccia, a timeless pleasure since 1885.
