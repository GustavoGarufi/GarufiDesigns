About me Laura Brophy

Laura Brophy is a worldly acclaimed Interior Designer based in Newport Beach, CA. With over a decade of experience designing countless homes, Laura cultivates environments that are personal, luxurious and livable.

Known for her first-class service and approachability, Laura immerses herself in every element of a client’s lifestyle when taking on a project. She considers her work to be a true curation and discovery of each individual client allowing her to not only transform their environments physically, but capture a beauty and spirit from within – one that transcends trends and continues to inspire for a lifetime.

Channelled through her innate sense of style, each project reflects the client's personality and vibe, resulting in an outcome that is truly personal and distinct. Laura helps clarify and capture her clients vision while staying true to their environment and lifestyle.
