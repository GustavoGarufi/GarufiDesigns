How we work about us

Our team takes a thoughtful, unique approach to each project we embark upon and each client with whom we work. While the design process may vary depending on a number of factors - including your budget, your unique need & concerns, and the scope of your project - working with Laura Brophy Interiors will typically include the following phases:

1. Initial Consultation

2. Design Concept Development

3. Furniture and Materials Selection

4. Order Placement

5. Installation

6. The finishing touches


*Depending upon the scope and urgency of your project, our Next Layer service may be the right fit for you. Over the last decade, we have successfully redesigned, furnished, and accessorized dozens of spaces in as little as two business days. For additional information, please contact us
