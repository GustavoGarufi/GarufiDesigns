Vision

Key Major Coin is positioning it's network members for the coming foundational shift in economics. As the Internet of things grows and payment systems develop, the machine economy, will in time, expand faster than the human economy. Along the way wealth creation opportunities will drive migration from fiat to crypto-assets. In many ways the rules of 21st century economics have yet to be written.
